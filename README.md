My website is my portfolio, something I want to expand upon in the coming years either by tweaking this particular site or starting again from scratch. I went with a blue to yellow to orange color palette because I like the color blue and wanted other colors that both pair well and contrast with each other for readability and color design purposes. I chose rounder fonts to make my site feel more casual and approachable by potential clients. I had issues getting the images to fit inside the cards, and resolved this by putting object-fit: contain in the .card section of my css.
